Seismica is an app for visualizing seismic data from IRIS web services: http://service.iris.edu/.

  • Put dates and time into form and click 'start' button. The earthquakes from this period will show on the map. Now you can choose the earthquake and see the seismic wave from this earthquake and additional data.
  • When you click and hold on the map with mouse movement from left to right you can spin the map.
  • If you are waiting too long for a data to show on the map try to send form with different dates or magnitudes to narrow the data. It seems like there is too much to load.
  • You can save earthquake data and wave for later to make comparison of different earthquakes.
  • Not always data is available. Especially for old events there are no seismic data in the database. You will see alert when this happen.
